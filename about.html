About the Echo

Somewhere between the deletion of a file and the next software update, a seam opened. We listened. We stitched the silence into fabric.

Silence//Echo is a small collective designing wearable artifacts from digital residue. Every piece is produced in limited runs, using recycled or low-impact materials, and ships in compostable packaging.

Our garments are meant to carry the memory of obsolete code—proof that even erased data leaves a shadow.
